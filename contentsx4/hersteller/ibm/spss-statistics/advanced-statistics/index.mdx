<Intro>

IBM SPSS Advanced Statistics ist ein Zusatzmodul für die Analyse mit univarianten und multivarianten Verfahren. Das Modul ist ab [SPSS Standard Edition](/hersteller/ibm/spss-statistics/standard/) und in der Subscription [Custom Tables & Advanced Statistics](/hersteller/ibm/spss-statistics/custom-tables-advanced-statistics/) enthalten.

</Intro>

## Funktionen

<ArrowList>

- Cox regression
- General linear modeling (GLM), general factorial, multivariate (MANOVA), repeated measures, variance components
- Generalized linear models and generalized estimating equations, gamma regression, Poisson regression, negative binomial
- GENLOG for loglinear and logit
- Generalized linear mixed models (GLMM) (ordinal targets included)
- Bayesian statistics
- Hierarchical loglinear models
- Kaplan-Meier
- Linear mixed-level models (aka hierarchical linear models)
- Survival
- Variance component estimation

</ArrowList>
